Charity Event for Villagers

Join us for a day of giving, as volunteers come together to support the village through donations, food drives, and essential services. Every contribution makes a difference in the lives of those in need.

Organized by Volunteers

Event Highlights

  • Donation Drive

    Contribute clothing, food, or funds to support the villagers in need.

  • Free Medical Checkups

    Volunteers and medical professionals offer health checkups for villagers.

  • Food Distribution

    Enjoy free meals provided by local donors and volunteers.
